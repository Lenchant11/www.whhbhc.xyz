Our Mission

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Hiwork Canada

How quickly will I hear back after contacting support?

Our support team typically responds within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please include "URGENT" in your subject line.

Do you offer services outside of Canada?

Currently, we focus exclusively on connecting Canadian residents with Canadian employers. All applicants must be legally eligible to work in Canada.

Can I speak with someone directly on the phone?

Yes! We offer phone support for complex issues. Please email us first with your phone number and availability, and we'll schedule a call.

How do you verify employers on your platform?

We conduct thorough background checks, verify business registration, confirm Canadian presence, and validate employment history before allowing any company to post jobs.
